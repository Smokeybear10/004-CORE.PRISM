Prism animation — five variants

Pick one and I'll lock it into the intro.

A★ · Anatomized refraction

RECOMMENDED · the move IS the 5 factors
The incoming beam is itself stacked from 5 colored sub-bands — the prism doesn't create the dimensions, it separates them. Output beam thickness = weight (real numbers: AMD 2021-11-08 Opus output: Mgmt 36%, Pricing 24%, Macro 23%, Competitive 17%, Demand 0%). What it says: "the move always contained all five factors, in these proportions. The model reads them out."

A · Classic refraction

Editorial · static aesthetic
Single beam enters → hits triangular prism → 5 colored beams refract. Loops every ~3 sec. What it says: "decomposition." Best for editorial / mentor-deck use.

E · Continuous particle stream

Living / dashboard feel
A constant stream of "moves" flow into the prism; each particle randomly refracts to one of 5 dim colors based on weight. What it says: "the system is always running." Best as a hero element on a dashboard or live demo.

G · Bar morph

Cleanest · brand-aligned
A PRICE MOVE →
DEMANDPRICINGCOMPETITIVEMGMTMACRO
A solid navy bar (the move) splits into the 5-color spectrum (the dimensions). Cycles every 4 sec. What it says: "one input → five reads." Most readable from a distance, matches the masthead spectrum, no SVG math required.

H · Wavelength refraction

Physics metaphor
Beams render as actual sine waves (literal "wavelengths"). Each dim has a different frequency, so they refract at different angles. What it says: "different signals bend differently — that's why we decompose." Most science-y, least corporate.

R · Reverse synthesis

Inverts the metaphor
Five dim streams converge into the prism → emit one verdict beam ("STRUCTURAL"). The prism becomes a synthesis engine, not a splitter. What it says: "we read five signals and decide." Better fit for the "lean/fade decision" framing than the "decomposition" framing.

T · Ticker feed (multi-input)

Story · scale
Multiple ticker labels (AMD / ABT / APD / AIR / ACU) spawn beams at staggered times, each split is tagged with the dominant dimension and a verdict color. What it says: "we run this on every move, across every ticker." Best for the stats-reel moment of a deck.
