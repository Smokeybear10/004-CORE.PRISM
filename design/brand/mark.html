PRISM · Waterfall P · Refinement

Four width patterns. Same silhouette, different cascade.

All four are five horizontal bands stacked into a P. The difference is how the bowl-bands cascade in width — that's the "attribution waterfall" half of the mark. Tell me which V to commit to (or which two to combine), and we move into brand.html.
